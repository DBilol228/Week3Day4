# Week3Day4
How It Works
The service simplifies cross-border shopping into four straightforward steps:

Get Your UK Address: Upon registration, OKEYPOST assigns you a unique, personal suite number at their secure UK warehouse.

Shop Online: Browse your favorite UK and European online stores (e.g., ASOS, Next, Zara, Amazon UK) and use your OKEYPOST UK address at checkout.

Warehouse Processing: OKEYPOST receives your packages, inspects them, takes photos (optional), and stores them securely.

Consolidation & Shipping: Combine multiple orders into a single box to save up to 80% on international shipping fees, then request delivery straight to Russia.

Key Features
Package Consolidation: Merge multiple small parcels from different stores into one optimal box to drastically reduce shipping overhead.

Repacking Service: Removal of unnecessary retail packaging and shoe boxes to lower the volumetric weight and save you money.

Storage Window: Free storage at the UK warehouse for up to 30–45 days, giving you plenty of time to wait for all your purchases to arrive.

Customs Declaration Help: Simplified tools and guidance to help fill out required customs documentation for seamless entry into Russia.

Mailout Optimization: Flexible shipping methods tailored to different budgets and speed preferences (Post/Courier networks).
